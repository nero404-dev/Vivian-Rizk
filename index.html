About

Know More About Me

I'm Dr. Rizk, Vivian Rizk. A university lecturer and researcher with a PhD in Mathematics, obtained through joint programs with Sorbonne Université, Université de Technologie de Compiègne (UTC) and Lebanese University.

My academic interests focus on Applied Mathematics, Numerical Analysis and Partial Differential Equations.

Since 2019, I've been teaching at the Lebanese International University (LIU), where I combine theory and application to make complex mathematical concepts accessible to students.

My ultimate goal? To help you achieve your best and exceed your targets regarding Mathematics.

Teaching

List of Courses I Teach

I teach a variety of undergraduate and graduate mathematics courses, focusing on helping students bridge abstract concepts with real-world applications. My approach emphasizes analytical thinking, problem-solving, and clarity in mathematical communication.

Research

Areas of research, current work and published papers / collaborations.

My research as a Doctor, focuses on developing mathematical models to describe complex physical and engineering systems. My work lies at the intersection of Applied Mathematics, Partial Differential Equations (PDEs) and Numerical Analysis.

Research areas:

  • Mathematical Modeling
  • Numerical Methods for PDEs
  • Fluid Dynamics and Heat Transfer
  • Computational Mathematics
  • Optimization Techniques

Selected publications:

  • “Numerical Simulation of Nonlinear PDE Systems in Fluid Flow” - Journal of Applied Mathematics, 2019.
  • “Error Estimation in Finite Element Methods for Heat Transfer Problems” - Applied Numerical Methods, 2018.
